Crop Disease Classification System 🌱Crop diseases are a major challenge in agriculture and can lead to significant losses if not detected early. Our Crop Disease Classification System uses image-based analysis to identify the health condition of crops and classify them into three categories:
Score 1 – Healthy Crop:* The crop is healthy and suitable for harvest or sale.
Score 2 – At-Risk Crop:* The crop shows early signs of disease but can still be treated and saved with proper care.
Score 3 - Reject Damaged Crop:* The crop is severely affected by disease and is no longer usable.By automatically analyzing crop images, this system helps farmers quickly assess crop health and take timely action. Early identification allows farmers to apply treatments when they are most effective, reducing crop loss and improving overall productivity.This project aims to support smarter and more efficient farming by using technology to assist in quick and accurate crop health monitoring. 🌾
